# Full Stack Engineer

End-to-end product development. Generalists who want to ship complete features.

---

## Foundation

`HTML / CSS / JavaScript` `Python or Node.js` `Git & GitHub` `SQL basics` `HTTP fundamentals`

---

## Core skills

`React` `Node.js / Django / Laravel` `REST APIs` `PostgreSQL / MongoDB` `Docker` `TypeScript`

---

## Advanced

`GraphQL` `Testing (unit & e2e)` `CI/CD pipelines` `Cloud basics` `Auth & security` `Performance profiling`

---

## Specialization

`System architecture` `DevOps fundamentals` `React Native (mobile)` `Real-time (WebSockets)` `Monorepo tooling (Turborepo)` `SaaS product patterns`

---

!!! tip "Key insight"
    Full stack engineers who can integrate AI features end-to-end are the most in-demand profile right now. Product thinking + AI plumbing = rare and valuable.
