# Deep Dive — Overview

The complete picture for each path: career levels, tools, learning resources, interview prep, and salary ranges.

---

## How to use this section

Each path page has five sections:

| Section | What you'll find |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Career levels** | What's expected at Junior, Mid, Senior, and Staff/Principal — skills and focus areas |
| **Tools** | Core tools (use daily) vs. useful-to-know tools, organized by category |
| **Learning path** | Sequenced, opinionated resource list with time estimates and cost |
| **Interviews** | The actual topics that come up, organized by category |
| **Roles & salary** | Job titles, years of experience, and approximate US compensation |

---

!!! note "Salary data"
    Ranges are approximate USD figures for the US market (2024–2025). Actual compensation varies significantly by company, location, and leveling system. FAANG/top-tier startups typically pay 30–60% above these ranges. Non-tech companies typically pay 20–40% below.
